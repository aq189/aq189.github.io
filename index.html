What is a TrailblazeHer?

A 12-week long career accelerator and personalized mentorship platform that allows high school girls to shadow & form a professional bond with female professionals in finance, entrepreneurship, and consulting.

"A TrailblazeHer is a woman pioneer, somebody who's willing to take risks and go in a path that isn't already there. They blaze a trail and leave a path for others."

Mentees

  • Career Accelerator

    A 12-week rotational summer program focused on providing mentees in-depth exposure to finance, consulting, and entrepreneurship. Each rotation will be 4 weeks long.

  • Mentorship Platform

    An 8-month long commitment during the academic year, in which we pair female professionals in entrepreneurship, finance, or consulting with four girls in their senior year of high school.

  • Resource Page

    Trailblazehers will have unlimited access to resume and cover letter guides, an internship board, and blog posts with content ranging from mentor introductions to work culture insights.

Mentors

Through this mentorship program, we hope to provide these young women with insight to entrepreneurship, finance, and consulting & foster a long-lasting support system.

Mentors will host bi-weekly calls with mentees during the academic year to discuss prompts that we will provide. In addition, mentees will have the opportunity to shadow their mentors for a day. During the summer, mentors will be invited to participate in a career panel.
